Mobility = Freedom

We are a non-profit organisation enabling disadvantaged individuals in the UK to enjoy the freedom of taking the train. Through our network of charity organisations we ensure that donations by travellers and the railway industry will end up in the hands of people that cannot afford to travel by train. These include:

  • Unemployed and/or homeless people
  • Orphans and impoverished children
  • Refugees

Your Tickets = Their Tickets

Order your train tickets as usual but add a donation. This will result in tickets for the most misfortunate people who cannot afford it. Charity organisations make sure that each of them is carefully selected.

Good CSR = Everybody Wins

Train Operating Companies could also contribute as part of their Corporate Social Responsibility strategies. They provide seats for these groups of people, ensuring that trains are at optimal capacity.
